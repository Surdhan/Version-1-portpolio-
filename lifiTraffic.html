LI-FI ENABLED SMART TRAFFIC MANAGEMENT SYSTEM FOR VEHICLE-TO-VEHICLE COMMUNICATION

Lifi Vehicular communication systems have emerged as crucial networks for enhancing road safety and traffic efficiency by facilitating information exchange among vehicles and roadside units. The alarming global toll of road accidents motivates the development of preventive systems. This study explores Visible Light Communication (Li-Fi) as a promising alternative to radio frequency-based prototypes, offering advantages such as high bandwidth and security. The paper reviews Li-Fi's working principle, emphasizing its use of visible light for data transmission through components like LEDs and photodiodes. The research delves into Li-Fi implementation in vehicular communication, Analysing prototypes, detailing methodology and components, presenting results, and discussing the project's future potential. The figure illustrates Li-Fi's working principle, positioning it as a transformative technology for next-generation traffic management.
