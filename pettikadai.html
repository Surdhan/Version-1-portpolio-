Pettikadai

TPETTI KADAI is a project aimed at empowering women entrepreneurs through a modern e-commerce platform. We compress traditional and modern methods to create a user-friendly application that enables individuals, particularly women's self-help groups, to sell their products efficiently. The application focuses on delivering high-quality, hygienic goods to customers while streamlining the purchasing process. Through PETTI KADAI, we seek to provide opportunities for women to enter the business world and contribute to their economic independence.
